# Code of Conduct

We follow the Contributor Covenant standard in spirit: be respectful, constructive, and focused on building reliable developer infrastructure.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep technical disagreement evidence-based.
- Respect maintainers' scope and review bandwidth.
- Do not harass, insult, or discriminate.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate issues, discussions, pull requests, or access when behavior harms the project or community.

For conduct concerns, contact `security@zroky.com`.
